Priorities

The EDC has established five piorities for economics and community development and - in response to community input - will focus the majority of our funding on a small number of large initiatives. Working Groups in most of these priority areas are developing proposals for our Major Grant Program, and we encourage members of the community to contribute by contacting the working groups.
